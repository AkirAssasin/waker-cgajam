WAKER

Waker is a bullet hell game with distinct line-art graphics and procedurally generated levels.

This is the original game, made for CGA Jam in 14 days (8th in Gameplay and 19th in Graphics).

The full game is in development and will contain:

  • a wider variety of colors
  • improved graphics and font, easier on the eyes
  • persistent levels
  • more mechanics
  • major balancing changes
  • narrative
  • proper tutorials and accessibility
